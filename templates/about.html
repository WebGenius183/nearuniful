About NearUni

Helping students find safe and affordable homes near campus

NearUni was created to solve one simple problem: finding safe, affordable student accommodation close to campus shouldn’t be stressful.

Every year, students struggle with fake agents, misleading listings, unclear pricing, and apartments that are far from their university. We believe students deserve a better, safer, and simpler way to find a place to live.

NearUni connects university students with available apartments near their campus, making the search process faster, clearer, and more trustworthy.

Our Mission

To help students find safe, verified, and affordable housing close to their university — without stress, scams, or wasted time.

What Makes NearUni Different

Student-focused

Built specifically for university students and their housing needs.

Campus-first approach

We focus on apartments close to campuses, not random locations.

Transparency

Clear pricing, real photos, and honest apartment information.

Trust & Safety

Listings are reviewed, and students can report suspicious apartments.

How NearUni Works

  1. Landlords list apartments near a university
  2. Listings are reviewed before appearing publicly
  3. Students search and browse apartments close to campus
  4. Students contact landlords directly to continue the process

Our Vision

We aim to become the most trusted student housing platform, starting campus by campus and growing with the student community.

NearUni is not just a website — it’s a tool designed to make student life easier.
